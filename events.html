TCCSA x UTMAS x PoC@Trin Lantern Festival Social

February 11, 2017 at 7 PM - 9 PM

Mid-terms and essays got you down? Come celebrate the end of Lunar New Year with TCCSA, PoC@Trin, and Utoronto Mahjong Society! We have yummy TANGYUAN (sticky rice balls filled with sesame or peanuts, traditionally eaten at reunions of family and friends) and MAHJONG and KARAOKE because it ain't a real party without Magi Jury serenading you with 90's Cantonese-rock! Mahjong is traditional tile base game that's super fun and the equivalent of the Chinese poker (It's how Kungfu Panda's dad won his noodle shop). We will have experts from the Mahjong Society teaching you how to play Taiwan style Mahjong! Come join the fun!

The Dumpling House

January 26, 2017 at 8 PM - 10 PM

This is a seperate event from the high table which is happening the day after at 6 PM. Come to both and get twice the amount of food! Dumplings are a traditional food consumed on Chinese New Year. They are a symbol of wealth and prosperity for the new year because of their similarity in shape to ancient Chinese currency!

Chinese New Year High Table

January 25, 2017 at 6 PM - 9 PM

新年快乐恭喜发财！金雞報喜迎新春!

Happy Year of the Roostr everyone! Come join the TCCSA and Trinity College in celebrating the year of the Rooster. This is the first time ever that Trinity College or any college in the University of Toronto has formally celebrated Chinese New Year - so come and enjoy the seasons festivities!

Kung Fu Hustle Movie Night

November 7, 2016 at 7 PM - 9 PM

Do you like Kung Fu? Do you like Chinese food? Are you stuck at Trin during fall break? If the answer is yes to either of the above, come to the TCCSA movie night we are showing Kung Fu Hustle by Stephen Chow! It will be super fun and enjoyable. Bring your friends! We'll have english subtitles available (and honestly the translation is funnier than the original dialogue). Afterwards we shall have a literary discussion on the symbolism of Stephen Chow's Buddha Palm technique.

TCCSA 举办 movie night 啦！经过大家的投票我们决定放的电影是：周星驰导演／主演的《功夫》！我们会提供饮料和点心，大家记得带上亲朋好友来看电影哦！ 语言：普通话 字幕：英语
