About Us 關於我們

Who are we?

The TCCSA is a network for U of T Trinity College students of Chinese background and those who wish to learn more about Chinese culture.


Our Background

Rising prestige and international recognition of the University of Toronto has attracted a greater influx of eager Chinese international students. As a result, the student body with Chinese heritage has become greater and greater. Despite, this growing presence of Chinese students on campus, the expected increase in international communication and exchange seems to lag. More Chinese international students choose to stay and confine themselves within the comfort of homogenous campus groups and miss out on all other parts of University of Toronto, for example - Hart House, college life and campus resources such as CIE, Career Centre. To challenge the status quo and improve communication between all student bodies, TCCSA is born - We hope to build a bridge that not only improves accessibility of university and college events to Chinese students, but we also provide domestic students with gain in international perspective from experiencing the culture of China, a new giant in the international playground.
