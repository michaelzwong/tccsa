About Us 關於我們

Who are we?

The TCCSA is a network for U of T Trinity College students of Chinese background and those who wish to learn more about Chinese culture.

Our Background

Rising prestige and international recognition of the University of Toronto has attracted a greater influx of eager Chinese international students. Despite the growth of chinese students , the expected increase in international communication and exchange seems to lag. More Chinese international students choose to stay and confine themselves within the comfort of homogenous campus groups and miss out on other aspects of university life. For example: Hart House, campus resources like CIE and the Career Centre. To challenge the status quo and improve communication between all student bodies, TCCSA was born.

Vision

We hope to build a bridge that not only improves accessibility of university and college events to Chinese students, but also provide domestic students with a chance to experience the culture of China, a new giant in the international playground.
